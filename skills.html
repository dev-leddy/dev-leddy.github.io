Blood Bowl 2020 Skills

Skill Description
BlockIgnore Both Down result during a Block; remain standing.
BrawlerRe-roll one Both Down result when Blocking (not Blitzing).
CatchRe-roll failed Agility test when catching the ball.
ClawsArmour breaks on 8+ when Blocking, regardless of target's AV.
DecayIf this player suffers a Casualty, roll twice on the Casualty table.
Dump-OffIf targeted while holding the ball, may interrupt to make a Quick Pass. Cannot cause Turnover.
DodgeOnce per turn, re-roll a failed Dodge Agility test. May also be used if targeted by a Block and a Stumble result is applied.
Frenzy*Must follow-up after a Block if able. If target remains Standing, must Block again (costs 1 MA or Rush). No second Block if a touchdown is scored. Cannot be combined with Grab.
GrabPrevents target from using Sidestep. When Blocking (not Blitzing), may push target into any adjacent empty square. Cannot be combined with Frenzy.
GuardCan assist Blocks regardless of being Marked by opponents.
Jump UpStand up for free if Prone. May attempt a +1 Agility test to Block when activated while Prone. Fails = remains Prone.
LeaderGrants one extra team re-roll if a Leader is on the pitch. Lost if no Leaders remain. Can carry into extra time. Not affected by Halfling Master Chef.
Loner (X+)*To use a team re-roll, roll D6. If result ≥ X, re-roll allowed. Otherwise, re-roll lost. Applies even if Prone or no Tackle Zone.
Mighty Blow (+1)After a Block, may add +1 to either Armour or Injury roll. Not usable with Stab or Chainsaw.
Nerves of SteelIgnore modifiers for being Marked when passing, catching, or interfering with a pass.
PassRe-roll failed Passing Ability test when performing a Pass action.
RegenerationWhen this player suffers a Casualty, roll D6. On 4+, they recover and are placed in Reserves.
SidestepWhen pushed back, choose any adjacent empty square instead of opponent choosing. Cannot use if no empty adjacent squares.
Stand FirmMay choose not to be pushed back. Does not prevent Frenzy second Block.
Sure HandsRe-roll failed attempts to pick up the ball. Strip Ball cannot be used against this player.
TentaclesWhen a marked opponent tries to move away, roll D6 + own ST – opponent ST. 6+ or natural 6 = hold them. 5– or natural 1 = fail. Only one Tentacles user may roll per escape attempt.
Thick SkullKO only on 9+ (8+ if Stunty). Lower rolls = Stunned. Other results unchanged.
WrestleOn Both Down, both players go Prone instead of applying normal result, regardless of other Skills. (not a turnover)
Vicious Vines(Maple Highgrove) Once per half, Maple may Block a player 2 squares away. Not part of a Blitz. No follow-up.
Savage Mauling Once per game, when Wilhelm Chaney makes an Injury roll against an opposing player, he may choose to re-roll the result.
